Terms and Conditions

Welcome to the Elite Football Series! We extend a warm welcome to all participants of the Elite Football Series, an exciting football tournament that brings together players, teams, and football enthusiasts from across the region. By engaging with this document, you agree to comply with our terms and conditions. These rules are designed to ensure the tournament runs smoothly, promotes fair play, and offers an enjoyable experience for everyone involved. Please read this document carefully, as it contains essential information regarding payments, attendance, dress codes, and player eligibility.

1. Payment Terms

1.1 Registration Fee Payment Requirements

  • All registration fees must be paid either before the match or immediately prior to its commencement.
  • Payments must be made in cash. Digital payment methods or checks are not accepted.
  • Under no circumstances will payments be accepted during the match or after it has started.
  • The team leader, captain, or any representative of the team may make the payment on behalf of the entire team.

1.2 Consequences of Non-Payment

  • Teams that do not pay will be disqualified from their scheduled match and will not be allowed to participate further.
  • No refunds will be issued for teams disqualified due to non-payment.

2. Arrival and Attendance Requirements

2.1 Timely Arrival at the Venue

  • Teams are suggested to arrive at least 30 minutes before the match start time.
  • Late arrivals disrupt the tournament schedule and are strongly discouraged.

2.2 Player and Substitute Attendance

  • Each team is required to bring all registered players, including substitutes, to the venue.

2.3 Starting Without Delays

  • Matches will begin at the scheduled time, regardless of late arrivals.
  • No goals will be awarded to either team until all players from the late-arriving team are present on the field.

3. No-Shows and Forfeitures

3.1 Consequences of a No-Show

  • The opposing team will automatically advance to the next round.
  • The absent team will not receive a refund of their registration fee.

3.2 Preventing Forfeiture

  • Teams should confirm attendance with all players in advance of match day.

4. Team Composition and Player Requirements

4.1 Minimum Number of Players

  • Each team must field the required number of players as specified in the tournament rules.

4.2 Fixed Fee Policy

  • The registration fee is fixed and does not vary based on the number of players a team brings.

5. Dress Code and Uniform Requirements

5.1 Matching Uniforms

  • All players on a team must wear matching uniforms of the same color and design.

5.2 Penalties for Non-Compliance

  • Teams failing to adhere to the uniform requirements may face penalties, including disqualification.

6. Player Eligibility

6.1 Registration and Age Limits

  • All players must be registered and meet the tournament's age criteria.

6.2 Consequences of Ineligible Players

  • Teams using unregistered or ineligible players will be disqualified without refunds.

7. Match Protocols

  • Teams should arrive at least 30 minutes early for preparation and warm-up.
  • Matches will not be delayed for late arrivals.

8. Fair Play and Sportsmanship

  • All participants must uphold principles of fairness and respect.

9. Photography and Media Consent

  • By participating, players consent to the use of their images for promotional purposes.

10. Risks and Liability

  • Participants acknowledge the risks involved in football and agree not to hold organizers liable for injuries.
